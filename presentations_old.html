MIT Physics (undergrad)

MIT Museum Girls Day Presentation

November, 2017

The MIT Museum hosts a Girls Day twice a year. In November 2017, I was invited to give a talk about physics I have done as an undergraduate. I gave my presentation to kids age 10 and older on the Optical Trapping experiment I completed in MIT's Junior Laboratory class.

Junior Laboratory (8.13) Presentation

May, 2017

Junior Laboratory (8.13) is an experimental physics class offered to upperclassmen in the MIT Physics Department. At the end of the semester I gave my public oral presentation on the Optical Trapping experiment, a biophysics experiment which uses a LASER "trap" to measure the force of myosin proteins in onion cells.
